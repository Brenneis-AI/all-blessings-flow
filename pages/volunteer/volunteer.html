Volunteer With All Blessings Flow

We have a volunteer role to match your interests and your schedule — whether that's a few hours a week or a one-time project.

Where You Could Help

Repair Assistance

Help repair used medical equipment like wheelchairs and rollator walkers. Mechanical or handyman skills helpful; flexible hours, including outside our open hours.

Warehouse Assistance

Assist clients with equipment requests, receive donations, and help keep the warehouse organized. Requires walking and lifting; at least one day a week.

Pickup & Delivery

Have a truck? Help us pick up and deliver larger equipment like hospital beds to families who don't have transportation.

Client Assistance & PR/Marketing

Help with client intake, or lend a hand with our website and social media presence.
