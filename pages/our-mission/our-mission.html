Our Mission: One Blessing Becomes Another

All Blessings Flow is a local nonprofit built on a simple idea: high-quality medical equipment often sits idle in closets while families struggle to afford the care tools they need. We collect it, refurbish it, and pass it on — free of charge — to neighbors across Central Virginia who need it.

Since 2015, that idea has become 52,000+ families served, 214,000+ items given out, and $13.4M+ saved for the people who needed it most.

One blessing becomes another — all blessings flow.

Started by Neighbors, for Neighbors

All Blessings Flow was founded in 2015 by Annie Dodd, who serves as President & CEO, alongside co-founder Douglas Dodd, who serves as Treasurer.
