Give Your Equipment a Second Life

If you have gently used medical equipment you no longer need, someone in Central Virginia needs it right now. Give it a second life.

What We Can Take

Walkers, rollators, canes & crutches
Wheelchairs & power wheelchairs
Shower chairs, tub benches & commodes
Bed rails, reachers & patient lifts
Unopened wound care supplies & adult briefs
CPAP & BiPAP machines
Recliner lift chairs
Standard electric hospital beds

What We Can't Take

Non-repairable equipment, manual or specialty hospital beds, oxygen tanks, medicines, used CPAP masks or tubing, and expired nutritional supplements.

Drop-Off Info

Drop off gently used equipment at our Charlottesville warehouse during open hours, or at our Elkton or Palmyra locations.
