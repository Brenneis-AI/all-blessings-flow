How to Receive Support

We make the process simple, dignified, and entirely free of cost for those in need.

1. Request Equipment

Fill out our online request form or call one of our locations to tell us what you need for your recovery or care.

2. Get Matched

We review our inventory of donated wheelchairs, hospital beds, and walkers to find the right equipment for your needs.

3. Pick Up for Free

Once matched, visit your nearest location to collect your equipment. There is never any charge or hidden fee.

One blessing becomes another.

All Blessings Flow began with a simple observation: high-quality medical equipment often sits idle in closets while families struggle to afford the care tools they need. We bridge that gap.

Every item donated to us is cleaned, inspected, and passed forward to another person in our community — continuing a cycle of care that has touched tens of thousands of lives across Central Virginia.
